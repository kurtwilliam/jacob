Hi, I'm Jacob.

I grew up in the north (meaning Sudbury) close to nature and the harsh realities that come with it. I love capturing the raw beauty of forests and wildlife. Since moving to the big city (Toronto) I’ve focused my lens on capturing the nature of the city and the thriving life it supports, from cold metallic structures to the vibrant warmth of lives within. I try to capture stories or moments, and since a picture can tell a thousand words, I’ve told a lot of stories.
